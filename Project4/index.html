About

The Niche Design Conference is an event to discuss the numerous unique design jobs and positions available to up-and-coming designers. 3 speakers will discuss their unique jobs in the design field and talk about their craft and career.

Location: Baltimore Convention Center

Date: July 10th

10am-4pm

Speakers

Annie Atkins

Graphic Designer for films such as Grand Budapest Hotel, and Boxtrolls.

Nicole Lazzaro

Nicole Lazzaro is a world-renowned game developer / designer who pioneered measuring emotions on players faces as they play.

Kyle Bean

Designer and 3d illustrator who works with unqiue handcrafted designs.

Schedule

10:30am - 11:45am - Annie Atkins: The Secret World of Design for Film

12:15pm - 1:15pm - Nicole Lazzaro: Using Emotions to Create Engaging Design

2:00pm - 3pm - Kyle Bean: Applying Personal Style to Brand Work
